Map for Evaluating Supply and Demand of Children Park

Posted on 2018-12-17 | In Data Analysis

Tools: ArcGIS 1.2, R

This map was made to calculate and estimate about index of supply and demand for Children’s park in Jungnang-gu, Seoul, Korea.

To estimate the index, at first, my colleagues and I calculated demand index by using factors that might affect children-park usage. Second, my team calculated supply index by calculating container index that is one of the ways to calculate accessibility.

As a result, pale yellow in the map shows a paucity in supply-demand of children park and dark red represents an excellent in supply-demand of children’s park.

Reference
Park, Sohyun., Kim, Kyusik., and Koh, Byeongok, 2014, A study on the Estimation of Supply and Demand for Children’s Park, Seoul studies 15(3), 79-93..
