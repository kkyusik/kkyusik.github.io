Word Cloud of Chon-se in the Internet Portal News

Posted on 2018-12-17 | In Data Analysis

Data sources: Naver.com
Techniques used: Web crawling, Text analysis
Tools: Python, R

This is a Word Cloud that represents a frequency of a word in a group of documents. The documents I used is a result of web crawling of Internet news in Naver, which is the biggest portal website in Korea.

To implement this procedure, the words are collected by Python in Internet website. In sequence, the words collected are grouped by using R.

Word Cloud means the frequency of word which represents from small to big. Therefore, you can perceive the importance of the word in documents in which you are interested. In this case, it shows that people are interested in rental prices.
